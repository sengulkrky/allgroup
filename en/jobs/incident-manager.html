Incident Manager

As an Incident Manager, you are responsible for handling and resolving IT incidents to ensure stability and continuity of services. You coordinate teams, help resolve disruptions, and communicate clearly with all stakeholders.

You are part of an IT team where responsiveness, collaboration and quality are priorities, and you contribute to improving processes and incident prevention.
